Extractive Text Summarization

There are so many articles all over the internet but are all of them worth your time? Extractive Summarization let's one get a gist of what a piece of text or an article has to offer to it's readers!
In Extractive Text Summarization important information or words are extracted from a given text and are ranked, then a constructive sentence is formed.

NLP - Extractive Text Summarizer - Built using nltk package - Flask backend - HTML/CSS/JS.
